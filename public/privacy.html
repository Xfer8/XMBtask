Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 29, 2026

XMBtask is a free task management tool made available to a small group of trusted colleagues by its developer. This policy explains what information is collected, how it is stored, and how it may be used in the future.

Who Can Use This App

Access to XMBtask is invitation-only. Users must be explicitly added to an access list by the app developer before they can sign in. Access may be requested through the sign-in page and is granted at the developer's discretion.

Information We Collect

When you sign in with Google, XMBtask receives your name and email address from your Google account. This information is used solely to identify your account and control access to the app.

Any data you enter into the app — including tasks, projects, notes, and related content — is stored in a secure cloud database (Google Firestore) associated with your account.

How Your Data Is Used

Your data is used only to provide the app's core functionality. At this time, your data is not shared with any other users, third parties, or external services, and is not used for advertising, analytics, or any commercial purpose.

The app developer has technical access to all data stored within the application as part of administering the service. This access will not be used to read, share, or act on your data without your knowledge.

Future Changes — Collaboration Features

A future version of XMBtask may introduce team collaboration features that allow data to be shared between authorized users. No such sharing will occur without advance notice and your explicit consent. All users will be notified before any change of this nature is introduced and will be given the opportunity to review and agree to updated terms before it takes effect.

Data Storage & Security

All data is stored using Google Firebase (Firestore), hosted in the United States. Access is secured via Google authentication and Firestore security rules that restrict each user to their own data. No passwords are stored by XMBtask.

Third-Party Services

XMBtask uses the following third-party services to operate:

Google Firebase — authentication and data storage. Governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

Groq — AI-powered text analysis for the Scratch Pad feature. Text you submit for analysis is sent to Groq's API. Governed by Groq's Privacy Policy.

Your Rights

You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting the app developer directly. Upon request, all stored data will be permanently removed.

Contact

If you have any questions about this policy, please reach out to the app developer at b10rok@gmail.com.
